The Problem

People of Color could miss their chance to vote in the upcoming Presidential election.

Dozens of states have made it more difficult to register and get to polling stations because of timing and location rules for registering and voting.

The Plan

Create a web application that introduces:

  • People willing to give lifts to registration offices or polling stations
